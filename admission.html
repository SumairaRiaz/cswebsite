BS(CS) - PROGRAM INFO

BS(CS) program is the undergraduate program offered at the Department of Computer Science. Both MORNING & EVENING Sessions are available from FALL 2021 onwards. It is a four-year Degree Program (Bachelor of Science in Computer Science) of 130 credit hours spread over 8 semesters.


Intermediate with at least 50% aggregate marks in any of the following: Pre- Engineering/Commerce, General Science/ Humanties with Computer or Mathematics, DAE with Electrical/Electronics/IT Technology, A-level with Computer/Mathematics. Pre-Medical, Subject to qualification of Additional Mathematics Subjects within 1st year of Program Enrollment*.
